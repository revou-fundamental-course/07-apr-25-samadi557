Our Profile

Hi there! I'm Samadi Prasetyo, a professional illustrator, caffeine enthusiast, and proud over-user of the "undo" button. I specialize in bringing bold ideas to life with hand-drawn charm, a sprinkle of humor, and just the right amount of "did-they-mean-to-do-that?"—(spoiler: I did). With a background in fine arts and a sketchbook that’s seen more drama than reality TV, I create visuals that pop, connect, and occasionally wink at you. From quirky icon sets to detailed editorial illustrations, I’m all about turning concepts into artwork that makes people stop scrolling.
